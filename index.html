ABOUT

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Duke University in the laboratory of Dorothy Sipkins, MD. PhD. I use intravital imaging to study breast cancer leptomeningeal metastasis in vivo, in real-time in mouse models of breast cancer. I am fascinated by brain tumors and neuroimmunology. Specifically, I am intersted in the role of macrophages in the brain tumor microenvironment and how they cooperate with T-cells. Please reach out for a complete CV.
